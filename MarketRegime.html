3-Way Alignment Methodology — adapted from theStrat Lab: "Why Market Regimes are a Swing Trader's secret weapon".
  • Market Mood: VVIX/VIX ratio smoothed with a 21-period moving average. Bullish when the ratio is above its MA and the MA's slope is positive; bearish when below the MA with a negative slope.
  • QQQ & SPY Cycles: Price action vs 21 EMA. Bullish requires the entire daily candle (low included) above the EMA; bearish requires the entire candle (high included) below the EMA. Straddling candles yield a flat/neutral state.
  • 3-Way Alignment:
    — Full Bull / Full Bear (3/3): Mood, QQQ, and SPY are unanimously bullish or bearish. Maximum conviction sizing favored.
    — Partial Bull / Partial Bear (2/3): Two signals align directionally while one is flat/neutral (e.g. Bull Mood + Bull QQQ + Flat SPY). Moderate size permitted; trend is constructive but lacking full participation.
    — Mixed / Conflicted: Direct contradiction between signals (e.g. Bullish Mood vs Bearish QQQ/SPY). Reduce size significantly or sit on hands until alignment resolves.
