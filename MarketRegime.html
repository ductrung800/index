Methodology — adapted from theStrat Lab: "Why Market Regimes are a Swing Trader's secret weapon".
  • Market Mood: VVIX/VIX ratio, smoothed with a 21-period moving average. Bullish when the ratio is above its MA and the MA's slope is positive; bearish when below the MA with a negative slope. Consecutive days in the same state build the streak.
  • Market Cycle: QQQ vs. its 21 EMA. Bullish requires the entire daily candle (low included) above the EMA; bearish requires the entire candle (high included) below the EMA. Streak tracks consecutive fully-confirmed days.
  • Alignment: Both regimes must agree — bullish + bullish, or bearish + bearish — before conviction sizing is favored. Mixed signals call for reduced size; use the EMA slope as tiebreaker (flat slope = sit on hands).
